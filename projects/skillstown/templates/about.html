About This Tool

What is the SkillsTown CV Analyzer?

The SkillsTown CV Analyzer is an AI-powered tool designed to help professionals find the most relevant courses for their career development. By analyzing your CV, the tool identifies your current skills and suggests SkillsTown courses that can help you enhance your expertise or develop new competencies.

How It Works

Our tool uses natural language processing (NLP) to analyze your CV and extract relevant skills and experience. It then matches these skills against SkillsTown's extensive course catalog to recommend courses that align with your professional profile and potential career growth areas.

Privacy & Data Security

We take your privacy seriously. Your CV is only processed temporarily for analysis purposes and is not stored permanently. The data extracted from your CV is used solely to generate course recommendations and is not shared with third parties.

About SkillsTown
